# Changelog

## 1.0.0 (beta)
- Initial public release for WoW Classic Era (Interface 11507).
- 243 achievements across 12 categories; 37 wearable titles.
- Hidden achievements with first-discoverer credit (name + realm).
- Saga meta-chains for every race and faction, ending at "Legend of Azeroth".
- Custom achievement window, Classic-styled toasts, on-screen tracker.
- Peer-to-peer sync, right-click inspect, guild/alt leaderboard, statistics.
- Speed runs, /played speed-leveling, deathless feats with install-time failsafe.
- Rare Radar, auto-screenshots, /ia flex, export/import, bug report window.
